    PM set to lay foundation stone of Jewar Airport

    Nov 27, 2019

    With the presentation of UP’s budget for 2019-2020, there is a lot to be excited for. Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath’s government has decided to allot a massive amount of INR 800 crores for a new project, the Jewar Airport.

    The airport, even before the construction has commenced, has become the talk of the town since it is going to be the second airport in Delhi-NCR and is expected to change the landscape of Noida’s real estate.

    Jewar is located in Greater Noida’s Gautam Buddh Nagar which has witnessed the emergence of many high-flying real estate projects on Noida Expressway in the last decade and an airport will only heighten investments in the area.

    According to sources, the construction work will start soon after the earmarked amount will reach the account of the concerned administration. As per Union Minister, Mahesh Sharma, the ceremony to inaugurate the work is scheduled for the end of February and the Prime Minister, Narendra Modi will lay the foundation stone for the Jewar Airport.
